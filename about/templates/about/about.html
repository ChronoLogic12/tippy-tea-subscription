About us

Established in 2018, here at Tippy we have always held quality, diversity and fairness at our core. We We know that tea is an important part of many people's daily routines. For many of us it helps us relax, focus, and center yourself so we can get to all of the other important things. But finding great tasting, reliable, ethically and ecologically sourced tea isn't always easy. But it should be. Thats where we decided to step. It's out goal to take all the hassle out of the equation for you allowing you to get back to what's really important; Loving life, and great tea!
Here at Tippy we rely on our amazing team of tea experts to gather the best from all around the world to bring to you. From China and India to Kenya and South America, we test hundreds of teas from fair trade sources every month to make sure you only get the premium product. With each tea you receive you will also get a leaflet letting you know about this months teas and a brewing guide created by our experts so you know you're getting the most out of every leaf.
